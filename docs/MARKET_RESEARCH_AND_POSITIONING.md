## Q1: WHAT GROUP OF PEOPLE DO I UNDERSTAND DEEPLY?

### Primary Target Group:

**Indian Women (Ages 25-55) - Quality-Conscious Clothing Buyers**

### Detailed Breakdown:

1. **Working Professionals (Ages 28-45)**
   - Office-going women who need professional attire
   - Value comfort, quality, and appropriate styling for workplace
   - Have disposable income (₹5L+ household income)
   - Time-constrained, need efficient shopping experience
   - Frustrated with ready-made office wear that doesn't fit properly

2. **Homemakers (Ages 30-55)**
   - Daily wear and occasional wear needs
   - Value comfort and durability over trends
   - Often have specific body type challenges (post-pregnancy, age-related changes)
   - Prefer quality over quantity
   - Appreciate personalized service and honest guidance

3. **Festival & Occasion Buyers (All Ages)**
   - Need clothing for weddings, festivals, functions
   - Want to look good and feel comfortable
   - Willing to invest in quality for special occasions
   - Often need custom sizing for traditional wear (sarees)
   - Frustrated with one-size-fits-all approach

4. **Quality Seekers (Ages 35-55)**
   - Women who have been disappointed by fast fashion
   - Value fabric quality and craftsmanship
   - Willing to pay premium for quality and fit
   - Appreciate personalized service
   - Building a long-term wardrobe, not impulse buying

### Why We Understand Them:

- **Deep Cultural Understanding**: We understand Indian women's clothing needs, body types, and cultural preferences
- **Personalized Approach**: We listen to individual needs rather than pushing mass-market products
- **Quality Focus**: We prioritize fabric quality and comfort over trends
- **Trust-Based Service**: We provide honest guidance, not sales pressure
- **Both Customized & Ready-Made**: We offer flexibility to match different customer needs
